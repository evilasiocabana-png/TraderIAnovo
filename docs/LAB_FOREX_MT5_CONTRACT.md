# Lab Forex MT5 Contract

## Lab Responsibilities

- Choose theoretical setup.
- Choose decision timeframe.
- Provide theoretical entry state.
- Provide interest zone.
- Provide stop or exit model.
- Provide parameters for future MT5 reading and auditing.

## Forex MT5 Responsibilities

- Read MT5 status in read-only mode.
- Expose symbols, prices, and timeframe status.
- Never decide strategy independently.
- Never send orders.

## Report Responsibilities

- Consolidate Lab and Forex MT5 state.
- Make audit state visible.
- Prepare future position and trade records only after a formal mission.
